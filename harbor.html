Harbor

What it is:

A green-field project for ITS at Iowa State. It's purpose is to create development spaces such as databases and virtual machines quickly and easily within Amazon Web Services, without having to use those interfaces directly.

Time Frame:

September 2016 - Present

Where:

Information Technology Services (ITS) at Iowa State

What I learned:
