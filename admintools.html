Blackboard Admin Tools

What it is:

A middle-man piece of software that interacts between Blackboard (Iowa State's LMS) and Iowa State's information storage (mainframe).

Time Frame:

February 2016 - September 2016 (including Summer)

Where:

Information Technology Services (ITS) at Iowa State

What I learned:

I learned to be independant while working on this project. I would be given a task, and was able to complete full stack features including research and implementation. A large feature I added was the ability to create and manage cron triggers for several batch jobs Iowa State runs to pull data from itâ€™s mainframe. We had a full front-end interface that would allow creation, scheduling, rescheduling, and deletion of times when these tasks would run. I integrated the Quartz Scheduling framework into our project; no one on our team had any knowledge of the subject, and it was a great example of my ability to research and execute on new technology. Admintools was easily the best project I worked on. I was never treated like a student employee, and I genuinely did meaningful work.
